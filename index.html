F.A.Q

  • What are the dates and registration fees for Hackathon ?

    The main event will be organized on 6th and 7th of October. There is no registration fees for the event but Rs.100 per team has to be submitted while registering as an assurance amount which will be returned to the team onsite. Ideation round for teams Inside Pune will be held on 26th Of September.

  • Do I need to have any specific qualifications to be a participant for the Hackathon?

    You love to code, you are more than welcome to participate in the Hackathon.

  • What are the accomodation facilities and will travel allowances be provided ?

    Accommodation will be provided to all the teams coming from outside Pune.

  • Do we provide travel cost?

    We can’t guarantee reimbursements for everyone, but we don’t want money to keep you from experiencing Innerve. However, the maximum amount affordable by Innerve per team is INR 4000.

  • Ideation round details.

    Teams from outside Pune will submit their google drive ppt link while filling the registration form. A sample ppt template is available on Register --> Outside Pune Page .The last date for Idea submission is 15th September 2019 (For teams outside from Pune).
    For teams inside of Pune, Your Ideation round will be held on 26th Of September in AIT college itself. You will present your ppt in front of our Judges and if selected you will be invited for the main round.

  • What if I have never been to a hackathon?

    Then we're so glad INNERVE will be your first ever! It’s helpful to have some programming or technical experience, but it’s not a requirement. We’ll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.

  • Can I start working on my hack before the event?

    No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before Innerve begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand!

  • Sample PPT -

    It is not important to strictly follow the pattern but atleast see to it to get a gist. Download Sample PPT Template ↓

  • What can be the maximum size of our team?

    You can have maximum 4 members in your team.
